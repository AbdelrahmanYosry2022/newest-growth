About Growth Roots

Welcome to Growth Roots, your specialized partner in developing the meat and poultry industry.

Growth Roots is an Egyptian company and your specialized partner in developing the meat and poultry industry, with extensive experience in establishing and operating meat and poultry production factories (such as production lines for burgers, hot dogs, pane, and others). The company provides integrated solutions covering all food factory needs, from equipment and raw material supply to technical support and training.

Our Mission

Growth Roots aims to lead the field of providing comprehensive services to the meat and poultry industry in Egypt and the Arab world. Our vision is based on the values of innovation, quality, and continuous technical support for our customers. We believe that quality and food safety are the foundation of this industry, and we work to achieve this by adhering to the highest global standards in everything we offer.

Business Track Record and Trust

We are proud of the trust of major clients in our services; we have been honored to collaborate with leading brands in the Egyptian and Arab market such as Koki, Almarai, Rosso, Al-Dahan Group, Golden Poultry, Ain Shams Specialized Hospital, the Police, Shawarma Al-Reem, and others. This trust is a testament to our commitment to quality and punctuality, and reflects Growth Roots' position as a reliable and distinguished company in the food industry.

Our Growth Roots Family

Brothers Steel Company

Brothers Steel is the manufacturing arm of Growth Roots, specializing in meat processing equipment and poultry production lines, as well as industrial kitchen and restaurant equipment.

Ricatto Company

Ricatto is Growth Roots' specialized company for supplying high-quality flavor enhancers and raw materials used in meat and poultry product manufacturing. Ricatto offers a comprehensive range of food additives that contribute to improving flavor, texture, and extending product shelf life.
