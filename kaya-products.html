About Kaya Steel

Locally engineered production lines for world-class manufacturing

Kaya Steel designs and builds turnkey food processing equipment that meets European performance standards while remaining cost-effective and easy to maintain. Every system is tailored to your line’s throughput, space, and hygiene requirements.

Equipment Portfolio

Meat & Poultry Processing Lines

Complete deboning, mixing, and forming systems for high-volume meat and poultry production.

Nugget & Strip Lines
